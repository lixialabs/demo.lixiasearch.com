Thai Economy to Achieve Five pct Growth This Year

March 25, 2023
Thailand’s gross domestic product will expand five pct this calendar year, up from 3.8 pct in 1986, despite a projected slight decline in agricultural output due to drought, the Bank of Thailand said. Central bank spokesman Siri Karncharoendi told reporters the Thai economy clearly recovered in the first quarter from the lingering effects of the 1984-85 recession. He said the industrial sector is expected to expand 5.5 pct this year, up from five pct in 1986.
